Notes to Data Protection

Your confidence is very important for us. That’s why we maintain data protection and inform you of data received and stored, your future rights, lockout and deletion.

1. Data obtaining and accumulation

As the website is accessed the browser sends information on the type and version of the browser, operating system, Referrer-URL, IP-address, name data, access status, volume of data being transferred, date and time of request on the server. This data shall not identify a person or any other sources; they are first accumulated and then deleted after statistical assessment.

2. Cookies

Your browser additionally accumulates so called Cookies. In this case it is referred to a set of data to be provided by servers in Internet. Cookies may be deactivated in settings of your browser.

3. Inquiry, correction, lockout and deletion

Personal data may be collected only subject to your consent provided in responses to requests, regulation of contracts and technical management, and stored in places inaccessible by any third persons. Any data may be transferred to any third persons only for the purpose of fulfillment of the contract or subject to your consent which may be revoked by you at any time. Personal data transfer or sale is not carried out.

At any time, you may request information on the data being stored, the aim of storage and sources. Besides, according to the statutory provisions, you have the right to use, lock and delete personal data. A respective request or desire may be submitted using contact information specified on the website.
